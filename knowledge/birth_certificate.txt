# Birth Certificate Services - Government Guide

## Overview
The Birth Registration Office provides services for obtaining birth certificates, which are essential documents for proving identity, age, and citizenship.

## Types of Birth Certificate Services

### 1. Standard Birth Certificate Request
For obtaining a certified copy of an existing birth record.

**Required Documents:**
- Valid government-issued ID
- Completed Application Form BC-001
- Relationship proof (if requesting for a family member)

**Processing Time:** 5-7 business days
**Fee:** $25 per certified copy

### 2. First-Time Birth Registration (Late Registration)
For registering births that were not registered at the time of birth.

**Required Documents:**
- Hospital birth record or baptismal certificate
- Affidavit of two witnesses who were present at birth
- National ID of parents
- Completed Form BC-002
- DNA test results (if birth occurred more than 10 years ago)

**Processing Time:** 30-45 business days (requires verification)
**Fee:** $75 plus $15 late registration penalty

### 3. Birth Certificate Correction
For correcting errors in an existing birth certificate.

**Types of Corrections:**
- **Clerical Errors:** Spelling mistakes, typographical errors
  - Fee: $30 | Processing: 10 business days
- **Substantial Changes:** Name changes, date corrections, parent information
  - Fee: $100 | Processing: 30-60 business days (court order may be required)

**Required Documents:**
- Original birth certificate
- Supporting documents proving correct information
- Completed Form BC-003
- Court order (for substantial changes)

## Application Process

### Step 1: Determine Service Type
Identify which service you need based on your requirements.

### Step 2: Gather Documents
Collect all necessary documents. All documents must be originals or certified copies.

### Step 3: Submit Application
**Online:** www.gov.services/birth-certificate
**In-Person:** Any Regional Civil Registry Office
**By Mail:** Civil Registry, PO Box 1000, Capital City

### Step 4: Pay Fees
Accepted payment methods:
- Credit/Debit cards
- Bank transfer
- Cash (in-person only)
- Mobile payment (GovPay app)

### Step 5: Receive Certificate
- Pick up from office (bring receipt and ID)
- Home delivery available ($10 additional fee)
- Digital copy available via GovServices portal

## Special Circumstances

### Birth Abroad
Citizens born abroad to national parents can register their birth.

**Additional Requirements:**
- Consular Report of Birth Abroad
- Parents' passports and proof of citizenship
- Foreign birth certificate (translated and apostilled)
- Fee: $50

### Adoption Birth Certificates
Adopted children receive new birth certificates listing adoptive parents.

**Requirements:**
- Final adoption decree
- Original birth certificate (sealed)
- Completed Form BC-004
- Processing: 15-20 business days
- Fee: $40
