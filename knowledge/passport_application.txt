# Passport Application Requirements - Government Services Guide

## Overview
This document outlines the complete requirements and procedures for applying for a national passport through our government services portal. Citizens can apply for new passports, renew existing passports, or replace lost/damaged passports.

## Types of Passport Applications

### 1. New Passport Application (First-Time Applicants)
For citizens who have never held a national passport before.

**Required Documents:**
- Original birth certificate
- National ID card (original and photocopy)
- Four recent passport-sized photographs (white background, 4.5cm x 3.5cm)
- Proof of citizenship (citizenship certificate or naturalization document)
- Completed Application Form PA-101

**Processing Time:** 15-20 business days
**Fee:** $85 for adults (ages 16+), $55 for minors (under 16)

### 2. Passport Renewal
For citizens with an existing passport that is expired or expiring within 6 months.

**Required Documents:**
- Current passport (original)
- Two recent passport-sized photographs
- Completed Application Form PA-102
- Proof of address (utility bill or bank statement, less than 3 months old)

**Processing Time:** 10-15 business days
**Fee:** $70 for adults, $45 for minors

**Note:** If your passport expired more than 5 years ago, you must apply as a new applicant.

### 3. Lost or Damaged Passport Replacement

**Required Documents:**
- Police report (for lost passports)
- Damaged passport (if applicable)
- National ID card (original and photocopy)
- Four recent passport-sized photographs
- Completed Application Form PA-103
- Affidavit of Loss (Form PA-103A)

**Processing Time:** 20-25 business days
**Fee:** $110 for adults, $75 for minors (includes replacement penalty)

## Application Process

### Step 1: Gather Documents
Collect all required documents based on your application type. Ensure photographs meet specifications.

### Step 2: Complete Application Form
- Download the appropriate form from our website: www.gov.services/passport
- Fill in all fields accurately using black ink
- Do not leave any fields blank (write "N/A" if not applicable)

### Step 3: Book an Appointment
- Visit www.gov.services/appointments
- Select "Passport Services"
- Choose your preferred date, time, and location
- You will receive a confirmation email with your appointment number

### Step 4: Submit Application
- Arrive at the passport office 15 minutes before your appointment
- Bring all original documents and photocopies
- Biometric data (fingerprints and photo) will be collected on-site
- Pay the applicable fee (cash, card, or mobile payment accepted)

### Step 5: Track Your Application
- Use your receipt number to track status at www.gov.services/track
- You will receive SMS notifications at each processing stage
- Collect your passport from the designated office or opt for home delivery ($15 additional fee)

## Expedited Services

For urgent travel needs, expedited processing is available:

**Express Processing (5-7 business days):** Additional $50 fee
**Urgent Processing (2-3 business days):** Additional $100 fee

**Eligibility for Expedited Services:**
- Proof of travel (flight booking, visa appointment)
- Medical emergency documentation
- Death of immediate family member abroad

## Photograph Specifications

- Size: 4.5cm x 3.5cm (1.77in x 1.38in)
- Background: Plain white or off-white
- Expression: Neutral, mouth closed
- Eyes: Open and clearly visible
- No glasses, hats, or head coverings (except for religious reasons)
- Taken within the last 6 months
- Professional quality, no selfies

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I apply for a passport on behalf of someone else?**
A: No, all applicants must appear in person for biometric collection. Exceptions are made for minors (accompanied by both parents or legal guardian) and those with certified medical conditions.

**Q: How long is a passport valid?**
A: Adult passports (16+) are valid for 10 years. Minor passports (under 16) are valid for 5 years.

**Q: Can I travel while my passport is being renewed?**
A: No, your current passport is retained during processing. Plan accordingly.

**Q: What if my name has changed since my last passport?**
A: Submit legal documentation of name change (marriage certificate, court order) along with your renewal application. Additional processing time may apply.

**Q: Are there any travel restrictions with a nearly expired passport?**
A: Many countries require at least 6 months validity beyond your travel dates. Renew early.
